    AI-Powered Smart Pricing

    Booking Vision's Smart Pricing uses machine learning to optimize your rates automatically.

    How It Works
    • Analyzes market demand and competitor pricing
    • Considers seasonal trends and local events
    • Adjusts prices based on booking lead time
    • Factors in your property's performance history
    Setting Up Smart Pricing
    1. Go to AI Features → Smart Pricing
    2. Enable Smart Pricing for your properties
    3. Set your minimum and maximum price limits
    4. Configure pricing rules and preferences
